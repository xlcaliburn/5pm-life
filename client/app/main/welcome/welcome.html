What is 5PMLIFE?

Are you an art-lover trying to find people who share the same passion?
An aspiring entrepreneur looking for potential partners?
Or just someone looking for something to do this Friday night?

5pm is a new platform that connects you with other like-minded people through similar interests, activities and events. Rather than checking to see if an event works with your schedule, we reverse the process and create a custom event for you at a time and place that you specify. All you have to do is tell us what you want and we will do all the work for you. Try 5pm today and make some new friends today!

5PMLIFE gives you the platform to find like-minded people to:
